Our History

In 1953 Alberti's opened in Skokie in 1980 Randy Miles took over while he changed the name to Village Inn he kept the recipes and great traditions of a family pizzeria now and now he's brought these traditions to Glenview. The Village Inn over 50 years of tradition now in 2 locations.

Serving downtown Skokie 1990, The Village Inn Pizzeria Sports Bar and Grill has been the meeting place in Skokie for great food, sporting events, and the place for people to relax and enjoy themselves. Conveniently located in the heart of Skokie in the same location as the former well-known Alberti's. Owner Randy Miles has been dedicated to fast and friendly service, quality food and customer satisfaction.

We are truly famous for our pizza with our pizza dough and sauce made fresh daily and topped only with the freshest ingredients. We have the best pizza on the North Shore and all of Chicago for that matter! But pizza is not all we offer. We have much much more. With a menu that boasts 165 items to accommodate even the pickiest of appetites. Most of our menu is homemade and made to order in our kitchen including our delicious award winning chili.
